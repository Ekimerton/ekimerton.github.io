My take on a seattle takeaway classic - specifically the mayo-based salad dressing.

## Ingredients

* 1/2 cup mayo
* 2 tbsp rice vinegar
* 3 tbsp of sugar
* 1/2 tbsp of soy sauce
* bit of sesame oil
* 1 garlic

## Steps

1. Chop garlic finely
2. Mix everything and put into a sauce bottle or something
