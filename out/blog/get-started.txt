9:Tba5,<p>This document is your gateway to quickly launching your professional site using the 10X Portfolio, a modular and customizable kit built with Next.js and styled using Tailwind CSS.</p>
<h2>Overview of the Kit</h2>
<p>The 10X Portfolio is designed to be intuitive and flexible, offering you 'Lego-like' blocks to build your unique online presence. Here’s what you can find in the kit:</p>
<p><strong>Core Components</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>ProjectsSection</strong>: Showcase your projects with options for images and descriptions.</li>
<li><strong>ExperienceSection</strong>: Detail your work experience.</li>
<li><strong>EducationSection</strong>: List your academic qualifications.</li>
<li><strong>BlogSection</strong>: Ready to integrate with your future blog posts.</li>
<li><strong>AboutSection</strong>: Introduce yourself with a personal bio.</li>
<li><strong>Navbar</strong>: Put <em>really</em> important stuff here -- it'll be shown on each screen.</li>
<li><strong>Hero</strong>: The first thing everyone clicking on your website will see.</li>
</ul>
<p>These components are found in the <code>src/app/sections</code> directory and are easily customizable to fit your style and needs.</p>
<h2>Setting Up Your Portfolio</h2>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Initial Setup</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Make sure Node.js is installed on your system.</li>
<li>Clone the repository and navigate into your project folder.</li>
<li>Run <code>npm install</code> to set up your dependencies.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Running Locally</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Start the development server with <code>npm run dev</code>. This command compiles and runs the application locally.</li>
<li>Open <code>http://localhost:3000</code> to see your portfolio in action.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Customization</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Tailwind CSS is used for styling. It’s set up for you to easily modify the design through utility classes.</li>
<li>Dive into individual component files under <code>src/app/components</code> to tweak functionality or update styles.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>File Structure</h2>
<p>Here's a brief look at the key directories and files:</p>
<ul>
<li><code>components/</code>: Contains UI components like buttons, navigation bars, and specific entry components for jobs, projects, and education.</li>
<li><code>sections/</code>: Houses the main sections of your portfolio page, making it easy to manage and update content.</li>
<li><code>globals.css</code>: The place to add global styles or override Tailwind's base styles.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Next Steps</h2>
<ul>
<li>Detailed tutorials on writing blog posts and deploying your site can be found at <code>/blog/blogging-101</code> and <code>/blog/deployment</code>, respectively.</li>
<li>For support, please email me at ekim0252@gmail.com.</li>
</ul>
